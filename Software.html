By Antony Turbeville, President, Platinum Benefit Services, Inc. April 7th, 2017

Medicaid Asset Protection planning began for me in 1996, frankly, when very few attorneys considered this a worthy practice area. Beginning with one assistant and a clientele that came from word of mouth, my Medicaid practice grew considerably. As each case presented itself with unique facts, further understanding of Florida Medicaid guidelines became necessary to accommodate individual family dynamics and case circumstances. Over the past 21 years, I have implemented many different strategies; some commonly known and some created as a by-product of intense due diligence necessary to accommodate my clients’ needs.


Today, more than 6,000 cases later, my company, Platinum Benefit Services, Inc. has assisted families in protecting more than an estimated $750 Million of assets. We have also assisted Community Spouses in maximizing spendable income resulting from optimum diversion of patient income. Further, we’ve assisted large nursing home holding companies in remedying large scale Medicaid pending problems.

The Reasons


Why am I willing to divulge information, techniques, and strategies acquired over 20 years and thousands of cases prior to the 2015 UPL Opinion, that even seasoned Medicaid planning attorneys are unaware of? Because Platinum will undoubtedly generate more business by consulting with and teaching a large network of attorneys the art of high quality Medicaid Plan creation, who will, in turn refer administrative and application functions to our firm. While there are competent attorneys in the Medicaid planning field - many more are needed to properly serve our exploding senior population. Furthermore, the 2015 Florida Supreme Court opinion on the Unlicensed Practice of Law, as it relates to Medicaid Planning, set forth guidelines on what parts of Medicaid Planning are allowed for non-lawyers. These guidelines directly impacted Non-Attorney Medicaid Planning firms who chose to abide by the specifics of he Opinion, although many continue to violate it. Platinum however, has embraced the opinion and has prospered by focusing on plan implementation and application processing for attorney created plans. By offering our vast experience and knowledge of high quality plan creation to the legal profession, rather than directly to the consumer, we have created a much more efficient way of distributing that experience and knowledge to the seniors and families that benefit the most. We continue to relentlessly perfect the science of expediting plan implementation and application processing. This dual approach has become a “WIN” for all involved!


Finally, few, if any law firms are structured for timely and efficient plan implementation and application processing. A large and consistent case volume is necessary to have a “well oiled machine”. We are that “well oiled machine” that allows you to practice law and families to be confident that a timely approval will occur.
