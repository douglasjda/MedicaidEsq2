Application Tracking Software

During the past 21 years, Platinum Benefit Services, Inc. has invested hundreds of thousands of dollars into software infrastructure. Our Application Tracking Software was originally designed to update Nursing facilities and corporate holding companies on the status of all their Medicaid Pending Cases in our system - Application status, deficiencies in documentation, communication logs, document requests, Department of Children communications and Approval notifications.

This same Application Tracking Software is now available to our attornies as an invaluable component within the MedicaidEsq™ "Asset Protection and Filing System". We fully understand that an attorney's time is money. We also understand the importance of the attorney being aprised of the application progress for each client. Our system dramatically reduces the amount of time expended by the attorney while still keeping the attorney and staff completely informed of the case status.
